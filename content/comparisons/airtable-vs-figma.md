**2. Feature Comparison**

1. **Cloud-based Database**: Store and manage data in tables, forms, and views.
2. **Project Management**: Assign tasks, track progress, and collaborate with team members.

4. **Automation**: Create custom workflows and automate tasks using Zapier or Integromat.

6. **Mobile Access**: Access your database and projects on-the-go with the Airtable mobile app.

### Figma Features:

1. **Cloud-based Design**: Collaborate with team members on design projects in real-time.
2. **Prototyping**: Create interactive prototypes to test and refine designs.
3. **Design Systems**: Manage and maintain design systems, including styles, components, and variables.
4. **Collaboration**: Invite team members to edit designs, share feedback, and track changes.
5. **Version Control**: Track design changes and revert to previous versions if needed.
6. **Integration**: Connect with popular design tools, such as Sketch, Adobe XD, and InVision.
