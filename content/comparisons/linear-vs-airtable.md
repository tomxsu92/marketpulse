* Real-time updates and notifications
* Basic reporting and analytics

Airtable, on the other hand, focuses on database management, but also includes project management features like:

* Customizable tables and views for tracking tasks and projects

Airtable stands out in data management, offering:

* Advanced data management with formulas and rollups

Linear has limited data management capabilities, making it less suitable for complex data analysis and reporting.

### Workflow Automation

Airtable offers advanced automation capabilities with triggers and actions, allowing users to create complex workflows and automate tasks. Linear has basic automation capabilities, but they are limited in scope and complexity.

### Customization

Airtable is highly customizable, with features like formulas, views, and rollups that allow users to tailor the platform to their specific needs. Linear has limited customization options, making it less flexible for organizations with unique requirements.

### User Interface
