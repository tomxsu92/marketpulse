## 1. HubSpot CRM — Best All-in-One (Free Forever)

**Why it won:** The only CRM that gives you full features for free. Paid plans add marketing automation, not core CRM functionality.

**What we loved:**
- Actually free CRM (unlimited contacts, unlimited users)
- Intuitive contact timeline shows every interaction
- Email tracking and meeting scheduling built-in
- Excellent mobile app
- Seamless upgrade path as you grow

**What could improve:**
- Marketing automation expensive ($800+/mo)
- Can feel overwhelming with all features
- Support limited on free plan

**Real results:** A 12-person agency tracked $340K in pipeline within 60 days of switching from spreadsheets.

**Pricing:** Free forever for core CRM. Paid starts at $45/month for starter features.

**Best for:** Any small business wanting a free, powerful CRM that scales.

## 2. Pipedrive — Best for Sales-Focused Teams

**Why it won:** Built by salespeople for salespeople. Pipeline visualization is unmatched. If you live in your CRM, choose Pipedrive.

- Best-in-class pipeline view
- Activity-based selling methodology built-in

- Email integration works flawlessly
- Fast, responsive interface

**What could improve:**

**Pricing:** No free plan. Starts at $14.90/user/month.
