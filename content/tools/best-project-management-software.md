## 1. Notion — Best All-in-One Workspace

**Why it won:** Notion replaces 5+ tools (docs, wiki, projects, notes, databases) in one clean interface.

**What we loved:**
- Infinite flexibility — create any workflow
- Beautiful, minimal design
- Powerful databases with multiple views
- Excellent free plan for small teams

**What could improve:**
- Steep learning curve for advanced features
- Mobile app can be slow
- No native time tracking

**Pricing:** Free for individuals. Teams start at $8/user/month.

**Best for:** Teams wanting one tool for everything — documentation, wikis, and project management combined.
